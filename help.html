How to upload the Books ?

To upload the book you have to login first and then follow the following steps:
1). Click on "profile" button


2). Click on "Sell Book" button


3). Fill the book details and then click on upload



How to buy the Books ?

To Buy the book you have to login first and then follow the following steps:
1). Go on "home" page


2). For Ex] Click on "Engineering" Icon


3). search for the book and then click on Contact Seller

4). you will get"Seller Information"
